About Us

Welcome to our Coffee Business! At Turtle Coffee, we are more than just a coffee company. We are driven by a deep commitment to environmental conservation, specifically focused on the protection and preservation of sea turtles. Our mission goes beyond serving exceptional coffee; we aim to make a positive impact on our planet.

With every cup of coffee you enjoy, 60% of the revenue generated goes directly towards funding our ambitious sea turtle conservation project. We believe that businesses have a responsibility to contribute to the well-being of our environment, and we are proud to be actively involved in this cause.

Founder & CEO

As the founder of Turtle Coffee Shop 23, he has accumulated experience from part-time jobs in restaurants, services, and majors in marketing and business administration.
